Trueload Logistics Pvt. Ltd. (TLL)

At Trueload Logistics Pvt. Ltd., we deliver reliable, efficient, and technology-driven logistics solutions tailored to modern business needs. Built on precision, transparency, and customer trust, we ensure seamless movement of goods across regions.

TLL specializes in Full Truck Load (FTL) transportation, Over Dimensional Cargo (ODC) consignments, and multimodal logistics operations. Our fleet includes 50 container-mounted trucks for high-value cargo, supported by an extended network of 110+ attached vehicles, enabling us to handle shipments of every scale and complexity.

With deep industry expertise, our team understands unique customer requirements and delivers innovative, tailor-made logistics solutions—especially for unconventional, sensitive, or high-care cargo. Every shipment is managed with attention, efficiency, and accountability.
