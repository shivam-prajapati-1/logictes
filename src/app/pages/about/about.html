Who We Are - True Load

Founded in 1939, True Load is one of India's oldest and most trusted names in the transport industry. With a legacy spanning over 8 decades, we have evolved from a traditional trucking company into a modern, tech-enabled logistics powerhouse.

Our mission is simple: to orchestrate seamless connectivity across India. Whether it's moving raw materials to factories or finished goods to retailers, True Load ensures every mile is covered with precision, safety, and reliability.
